# Privacy Policy for RetireSmart

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

RetireSmart ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection

RetireSmart is designed to prioritize user privacy:

- **Financial Data:** All retirement calculations and financial inputs are processed locally on your device or via secure API calls. We do **not** store your personal financial data on our servers.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics) to improve app performance and user experience.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase (Google):** For crash reporting and anonymous analytics.
- **Gold Price APIs:** To fetch real-time market data.

## 3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted through the app.

## 4. Your Choices

You can opt-out of analytics tracking through the app settings or by disabling internet access, although this will limit app functionality (e.g., live gold prices).
